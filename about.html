E.B.Dalton

E.B.Dalton is a writer, weightlifter and foul-mouthed knitter living in Richmond, Virginia, under a willow oak tree controlled by the squirrel mafia. Send nuts.

She writes speculative fiction short stories and is working on her first novel, set on a luxury hotel orbiting earth. She is also founder of a spec-fic writing group that hogs tables at cafe write-ins, bakes rejection cookies and sneaks off to the beach every summer.

She is a graduate of the University of Virginia, an ordained minister and a big fan of James River Writers, in reverse order of utility.
